SecureWipe - Data Erasure You Can Trust

A custom bootable USB solution built for enterprises and IT professionals who require absolute certainty in data destruction. It securely and irreversibly wipes PCs, laptops, and Android devices while adhering to NIST 800-88 standards. Every operation produces verifiable, tamper-proof proof of erasure, giving organizations complete transparency, audit readiness, and peace of mind when retiring or recycling devices

How it works

  1. Identify device

    Detect or choose the target (PC HDD/SSD/NVMe or Android).

  2. Choose method

    Quick Erase, Cryptographic Erase, or NIST Purge (full sanitization).

  3. Wipe & verify

    Tool performs wipe, then verifies overwrite/cryptographic key destruction.

  4. Certificate

    Automatically generates a tamper-evident certificate with timestamp and cryptographic proof.
